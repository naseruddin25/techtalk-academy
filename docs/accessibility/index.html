Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to inclusive digital experiences

Our Commitment

TechTalk Academy is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users, including people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards to our website and learning platforms.

We believe that technology education should be accessible to everyone, regardless of ability. This commitment extends from our website to our learning experiences, where we provide multiple pathways for learners to engage with content.

Conformance Status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) defines requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. We strive to meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards across our digital properties.

We recognize that accessibility is an ongoing effort, and we are committed to regular reviews and improvements of our digital content.

Accessibility Features

We have implemented the following features to improve accessibility on our website:

  • ✓ Keyboard Navigation: All interactive elements are accessible via keyboard
  • ✓ Screen Reader Support: Content is structured with proper headings, landmarks, and alternative text
  • ✓ Color Contrast: Text and interactive elements meet WCAG contrast requirements
  • ✓ Responsive Design: Content adapts to various screen sizes and zoom levels
  • ✓ Clear Navigation: Consistent and predictable navigation throughout the site
  • ✓ Form Accessibility: Form fields include proper labels and error handling

Learning Experience Accessibility

Our commitment to accessibility extends to our learning experiences:

  • ● VR-Enhanced, Not VR-Required: While we offer immersive VR experiences, all core learning content is available through traditional, accessible interfaces
  • ● Multiple Learning Pathways: Learners can engage with content in ways that work best for their needs
  • ● Accommodations: We work with learners who need specific accommodations to ensure they can fully participate in our programs
  • ● Assistive Technology Support: Our learning platforms are designed to work with common assistive technologies

Browser and Assistive Technology Compatibility

Our website is designed to be compatible with the following:

  • • Modern web browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • • Screen readers (NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver)
  • • Voice recognition software
  • • Screen magnification tools

Known Limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, we acknowledge that some areas may have limitations:

  • ! Some third-party content or embedded tools may not meet all accessibility standards
  • ! Certain interactive simulations may require specific capabilities
  • ! Older content may not yet be fully remediated

We are actively working to address these limitations and welcome feedback to help prioritize improvements.

Requesting Accommodations

If you need specific accommodations to participate in our programs, please contact us as early as possible. We are committed to working with learners to identify and implement appropriate accommodations.

Feedback and Contact

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of our website and learning experiences. If you encounter any barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us:

Continuous Improvement

Accessibility is not a one-time effort but an ongoing commitment. We regularly:

  • → Conduct accessibility audits of our digital properties
  • → Train our team on accessibility best practices
  • → Incorporate accessibility into our design and development processes
  • → Review and respond to user feedback
